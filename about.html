Democratizing Independent Media

We build tools that empower writers to own their relationship with their readers directly.

Writers Deserve Better Infrastructure

Inkflow was founded in 2024 by a small team of journalists and software designers who grew tired of fragmented web technologies. Writers were forced to cobble together billing plugins, design builders, and subscriber tables just to distribute a weekly PDF link.

We decided to create a single cohesive platform. An integrated editor, responsive delivery engine, custom domains, and integrated discussion threads. Simple, fast, and secure.

Our Mission

To provide independent voices with the premium technical utilities needed to escape advertising algorithms, run sustainable businesses, and gather tight communities.

Our Vision

A media landscape where content quality is prioritized, subscription relationships are transparent, and writers own 100% of their database archives.

Core Values Driving Inkflow

Creator First

We prioritize features that improve writing ergonomics and subscriber revenue retention.

Radical Simplicity

No visual clutter. We keep settings panels simple and build components that work instantly.

Extreme Security

DKIM settings, automated spam blockers, and SSL controls protect reader lists securely.

Data Portability

Export your subscriber databases, posts history, and billing records in one click anytime.

Reliable Delivery

We route issues using premium SMTP server pools to maximize email folder hits.

Zero Platform Lock-in

You point DNS to our servers, keeping full search engine authority for your articles.
